A complete GST Filing Solution.

Zoho GST will help you file your GST returns much faster. Stay GST compliant and leave your tax filing burden to us.

Watch How to file GSTR-1

Here's why Zoho GST is a better way to file your taxes.

End-to-end compliance

Whether it's generating your GSTR1/GSTR2/GSTR3, calculating your tax liabilities and input tax credits, or filing your returns, Zoho GST adheres to the GST law and helps you stay GST-compliant.

Painless reconciliation

Zoho GST makes reconciliation simple by automatically matching your purchase transactions from your GSTR2 to your vendor's GSTR1. All you have to do is amend or approve transactions that aren't matched in the GSTN.

Secure filing on the cloud

We use OTP verification while uploading transactions to the GSTN, and digital signature verification while filing your GSTRs, so you can rest assured that your GST filing process is secure.

Detailed reports

We keep track of the total tax amount you have paid to the government in our cash summary, and the input tax credit available to you in our ITC summary, so that you're always up-to-date on all the critical numbers pertaining to your taxes.

Collaborate online

Give your CA role-based access to specific modules and enable permissions to view or edit information. You can either give your users predefined roles or create custom ones.

Get timely notifications

Get notified when a GSTR deadline is approaching or when a transaction has to be approved. We'll make sure you're always updated on the status of your filing process.

We leave no stone unturned.

Zoho GST helps you file your outward supplies as GSTR1, inward supplies as GSTR2, and consolidated return GSTR3.

  • CONSOLIDATED RETURN
  • OUTWARD SUPPLY RETURN
  • INWARD SUPPULY RETURN

File your GST returns in three simple steps.

1. IMPORT

Upload your sales or purchase transactions in .csv format, map the appropriate fields and import all your transactions in just a few clicks.

2. PUSH

Once you have imported all the transactions to the GST app, you can upload them to the GSTN for verification. You will also be able to check the approval status of these transactions.

3. FILE

The final step is to file your GSTR returns. You can verify your filing process by uploading your digital signature, and complete the process of filing.
